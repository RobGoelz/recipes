1. 2.5 Pounds Groud Meat
2. 1/2 Cup Taco Seasoning
3. 1 Cup Water
4. 3 Tablespoons Tomato Paste
5. Tortillas or Hard Shell Tacos
6. Cheese
7. (Optional) Lettuce
8. (Optional) Tomatoes

Brown the ground meat in a large pan or pot over medium-high heat. Drain any oil after browning.
Lower the temperature and add water. Incorporate the water and the meat.
Add the taco seasoning and tomato paste and stir the meat to incorporate.
Increase the temperature to cook off any excess water and then remove the meat from the direct heat.

Add meat to torillas and taco shells and add cheese, lettuce, tomatoes and other fixings as
desired!
